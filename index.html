Java and Me

Bio

My life as a Java developer began in elementary school. After reading a PC World magazine in a dentist's office at the age of 9, I developed a great interest in tech. I wanted to read any material I could get my hands on about the tech industry. My first interaction with programming was a C++ class in 10th grade. It was an awesome course and I learned so much. But I never saw any representations of people I could relate to who were in the tech industry. No one looked like me. As technology advances and becomes more ingrained in everyone's life, I was drawn back to this industry. I want to be the example for young Detroiters who's curious about tech but doesn't see a reflection of it in their community.
